About StoryCast

Our mission

StoryCast publishes media stories that are equally meaningful whether you listen, watch, read, or combine all three. We focus on community narratives and inclusive digital publishing standards.

How to engage

  • Browse all stories from the homepage.
  • Use keyboard navigation (Tab / Shift+Tab) to move through controls.
  • Open transcripts for reading or offline use.

Accessibility features

  • Semantic landmarks and heading hierarchy for assistive technologies.
  • Visible focus states for keyboard users.
  • Captions attached to all video media with `.vtt` tracks.
  • Transcript files for every story in plain-text format.
  • High-contrast text and controls aligned with WCAG 2.1 AA goals.
